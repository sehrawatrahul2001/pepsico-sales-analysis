# Dashboard Brief

## Purpose

The dashboard is designed as a PepsiCo commercial review layer. It helps leadership assess revenue scale, product concentration, market performance, and time-based demand patterns in one place.

## Core KPIs

- **Total Revenue**
- **Total Orders**
- **Average Order Value**
- **Average Rating**
- **Top Category**
- **Peak Month**

## Recommended Views

1. Monthly sales trend
2. Category contribution
3. State and city revenue ranking
4. Top products by revenue
5. Quarterly performance comparison
6. Order-value segment performance
7. Day-of-week revenue behavior

## Management Takeaways

- Revenue is commercially strong but concentrated.
- Winning categories and products should be protected through availability and execution focus.
- Leading markets should be used as benchmarks for second-tier growth states.
- The late-year decline should be validated before it shapes forecasting decisions.
