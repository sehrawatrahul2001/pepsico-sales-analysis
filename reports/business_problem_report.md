# Business Problem Report

## Project Title

**PepsiCo Sales Performance & Business Insights Analysis**

## Prepared By

**Rahul Sehrawat**  
**Assistant Manager (Operations) | Transitioning into Data Analytics**

## Business Context

In beverage-led FMCG businesses, revenue performance depends on more than demand alone. Product mix, distributor execution, market concentration, and timing all influence topline results. Transaction-level data exists, but leadership still needs a structured view of what is driving performance and where intervention is required.

## Problem Statement

The business needs visibility into:

- the categories and products carrying sales
- the markets driving or lagging performance
- the time periods that matter most for revenue planning
- the role of order-value mix in overall business quality
- the commercial risks hidden inside a strong topline number

Without that visibility, inventory, market focus, and sales effort can be allocated on assumption instead of evidence.

## Analytical Objective

This project was built to:

1. clean and standardize PepsiCo sales data from Excel
2. measure revenue performance across product, market, and time dimensions
3. use SQL to support repeatable business reporting
4. package the findings into a recruiter-ready and leadership-ready commercial story

## Stakeholder Value

The analysis is relevant for sales leaders, category managers, regional teams, and analysts who need a clearer view of revenue concentration and growth opportunity. It also demonstrates how Rahul Sehrawat brings an operations mindset into analytics by focusing on execution, planning, and actionability.
