# Final Insights Report

## Executive Summary

The PepsiCo sales portfolio is large and healthy, but it is concentrated by category, geography, and order-value mix. The upgraded workflow now supports not only descriptive review, but also planning conversations around channel mix, demand softness, and commercial prioritization.

## Five Key Insights

1. The business generated **INR 53.01M** in revenue across **197,430 orders**.
2. `Carbonated Soft Drink` and `Juice` contribute **INR 29.79M**, or roughly **56.2%** of total revenue.
3. `Karnataka` alone contributes **INR 5.46M**, making it the lead benchmark market.
4. `Premium` and `Enterprise` orders contribute **INR 36.01M**, equal to about **67.9%** of revenue.
5. Monthly demand peaks in **May 2025**, while the baseline forecast for **January to March 2026** softens to **INR 1.52M**, **INR 1.07M**, and **INR 0.63M** respectively.

## Three Business Recommendations

- Protect leading categories and products with inventory discipline and focused distributor execution.
- Use Karnataka as the benchmark operating model for expansion markets with similar demand potential.
- Improve revenue quality by migrating `Core` orders into `Premium` bundles, pricing, or channel programs.

## Business Impact Statement

Shifting **5%** of current `Core` revenue into `Premium` mix would create approximately **INR 752K** in higher-value revenue.
